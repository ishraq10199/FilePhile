About this project

Services such as Google Drive, Dropbox, MEGA, have revolutionalized the file sharing eco-system. But this came at the cost of privacy. Your files are at the mercy of some server that is overseas, and if you lose access to your account's credentials, gone are your family photos, your private documents, and all other things you value. You no longer would have the ability to unplug the machine. While there exists alternatives for storing your files offline, its not easy to host your files with friends and family for free, given that your files are large in size.


This project aims to reduce this reliance on online services that are out of your control, and have all your files share-ready with your friends and family. Your files should be with you. Not under the eyes of a foreign entity.


Google and other file hosting services allegedly use your data and perform analysis on it to train their AI. This is a violation of your privacy, and is not to be taken lightly. Your files should be yours. Should you not have full reign over how your information gets used?

We do not store any of your data, when you host your files. Your files are always private, and stop existing on the web when you want it to. This can be scaled to any proportion, depending on what sort of storage you have. There are no false promises, no data-mining, no AI training using OCR on your information. To ensure that you have it all private, all source code is available for you to see.

-(Our Motto)-


Your files should be yours to see, yours to use, yours to regulate and share.
